    Carrot Halwa Recipe
    Carrot halwa is one of the popular recipe around the world. It is one of the most searched Indian sweets on the internet. I am sure every one loves a good bowl of warm carrot halwa. Recently carrot halwa with vanilla ice cream has become a very popular dessert in every restaurants which is one of my favourite apart from jamun with ice cream.
      ingredients:
    1. 500 grams carrots
    2. 1/2 tin condensed milk(1/2 cup+2 tabelespoons)
    3. 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder or 1 teaspoon rose water
    4. 1 and 1/2 table spoon ghee
    5. 1/4 cup chopped nuts(cashews,pistas,almond)
    6. 2 tablespoons raisins
    preparation
    1. wash 500 grams of carrots thoroughly under running water.a little more or less is fine to to use,not a lot.peel and fine grate them.a food processor quickens the process of grationg. so if you own one,you can use it
       
      make sure you use a heavy bottom large pot or pan for this recipe as it helps to prevent the halwa from sticking to the bottom and burning. however a pressure cooker too works well
    2. on a low heat,roast pistachios,almonds and cashew nuts antill crunchy.remove them to chopping board,cool and chop them.or alternately chop the nuts first or carefully pulse them in a grinder.fry them in one table spoon ghee until golden and crunchy.set side. if you prefer to use raisns,you may fry about 15 min in ghee until plump.set aside
    3. next to the same pan,por 2 cups of milk and begin to heat it
    4. After heating milk Add grated carrots
    5. stir and cook on medium flame.keep stirring to prevent burning at the bootom. since i used a heavy bootom pan,i didn't have to stir very often.but i suggest consistently keep checking otherwise the halwawill stick at the bottom of the pan
    6. You will have be cautious always towards the end.especially when the milk has almosy evaporated
    7. Next add half cup+2 table spoons sugar and stir
      8.  
    8. suger melts and carrot halwa turns gooey once agin.stir and cook.when the moisure is half evaporated,add 1 table spoon ghee and stir well.
    9. stir and cook until the halwa thickens.just before you finish off,add half teaspoon cardamom powder
      And finally your halwa is ready
